NECRO NECROMANCER NECROMANCERS NECROS

Necromancers are dark mages who have devoted their studies to the properties of
the undead. They draw negative energy from the deceased and use it to their
goal. From their studies, they have learned to animate corpses and control it
as his slave. They are also able to fashion golems from the material they have
to do them services. At the height of a necromancer's powers, he is able to
disintegrate another with a simple point of a finger.
