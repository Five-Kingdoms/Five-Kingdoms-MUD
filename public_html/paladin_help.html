PALADIN PALADINS

Paladins are the embodiment of goodness. They are holy warriors who are
empowered by the gods of Good to bring forth goodness throughout the realm.
They are noted for their chivalry, the characteristics of kindness,
generosity, mercy, and honor. Paladins are able to learn an array of weapon
styles as well as defensive skills to aid them in combat and are given ability
to bring down their gods wrath upon their enemies. Endowed with healing powers
near those of the clerics to grant help to the needy, Paladins are capable of
both powerful offense and defense.

Paladins are usually looked at by other warrior classes as Religious Zealots
with an axe to grind.

Paladins of any level who fail to role-play properly may be outcast.

For more information, see HELP OUTCAST and HELP GOOD.
