DRUID

Druids are the purest embodiment of nature. The live to serve only nature and
the natural order. They are some of the most ancient of sages in Mirlan. By
nature, Druids are communers, as they use the forces of the nature to cast their
own spells of protection and healing. They can be powerful healers and quite
knowing the ways of natural herbs and healing remedies. A Druid is a very
smart companion to know, as they can provide certain types of healing even
those of the Healer profession cannot.

Druids are the predecessors of the modern Ranger class and while they have many
of the same combat abilities, they are much more disenfranchized and clerical
than that of the Ranger.

As a Druid's home is within the forest, while he is away from the forest, he/she
will experience yearning (HELP YEARNING) the longer he/she remains away.

A druid will find his guild masters residing within a forest only, as druids by
nature dislike population and the crowd of a city. They are rural creatures, and
come out of the forest very seldom if ever.

If you desire to join the order of Druids, you must be of NEUTRAL align and be
either a HUMAN or LYCANTHROPE ranger of no more or less than the 30th rank. Upon
reaching this rank, inquire at the Ranger Guild.

NOTE: YOU MAY NOT BECOME A DRUID WHILE IN A CABAL.

See also: HELP YEARNING
